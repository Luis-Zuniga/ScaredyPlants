THE SCAREDY PLANT

LEAF ME ALONE

WHAT IT IS

The Scaredy Plant is an anti-social robot inspired by sensitive plants that displays fear when interacting with its environment.
HOW IT WORKS
With 8 actuating leaves on a wheeled flower pot, the Scaredy Plant can shrink its leaves and run away from frightening stimuli like loud noises and human shadows.

OUR INS​PIRATIONS

Shame Plant
We are inspired by biology, kinetic art, and human interaction with robots. We liked the motion of the real-life ‘shame plant’, which gets its name because it folds up its leaves and shrinks away when touched. One of our main goals was to evoke emotion with our robot in a non-obvious way, and to have our project interact with the environment around it.​
